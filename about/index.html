Welcome to CHIPSTERS.EU!

This page is about IOT and projects based mainly on C.H.I.P., OrangePi and of course RaspberryPi.

In the beginning we will try to focus on the ongoing project: rSTATION - an audio streamer and cloud player, which can be controlled by voice or via remote air mouse. rSTATION is suitable for using without any touch screen or other additional devices, e.g. smartphones. Our goal is to build perfect audio streamer for people, who have vision problems (blind or with other kind of vision impairment).
