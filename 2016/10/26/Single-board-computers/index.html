Single board computers

Oct 26, 2016 in story, rank
Orange, Raspberry Pi and C.H.I.P.

Currently (Oct 2016) there are more then 60 SBCs[1] with open-specification and this number is growing fast!

this is our subjective rank on SBCs (Single Board Computers)

For our project’s needs, we have looked at almost all available for purchase single-board computers. We have checked if they were:

  • hacker friendly
  • Linux compatible
  • open-spec
  • low-cost
  • effective

According to our internal magic score algorithm, C.H.I.P., Raspberry Pi (especially model zero), and Orange Pi (especially model PC+) have the higher price-performance ratio.

Right now we are testing the preselected devices (see in the table below) with additional hardware and our software.
